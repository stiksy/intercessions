# Intercessions for Holy Communion Service

**Date:**

**Bible Reading:**

---

Let us pray for the Church and for the world, and let us thank God for His goodness.

Almighty God, we pray for Your Church, especially for our local congregation. May it continue to grow in faith, love, and service. We ask Your blessing upon our church leaders and all those who contribute, both directly and indirectly, to its mission. Strengthen them in their work and guide them in Your wisdom. We also give thanks for the renewed heating system, which brings comfort to our congregation, especially in these colder months.

We pray for the leaders of the nations, that they may govern with justice and integrity, seeking the good of their people. May they be guided by wisdom, compassion, and a true desire for peace and righteousness.

We pray for peace in all places afflicted by war, violence, and oppression. Turn the hearts of those who seek conflict towards reconciliation, and bring comfort to those who suffer because of war and unrest.

We lift before You all who are suffering from illness, those in pain, and those who are struggling in body, mind, or spirit. May they find comfort in Your presence and strength in their trials. Surround them with those who will support and care for them.

We also remember all who are in need—the poor, the homeless, refugees, and those who struggle daily to find food, shelter, and security. May they find relief through Your mercy and through the kindness and generosity of others.

Lord, You know what troubles our hearts, and what are our unspoken needs. You see our joys and our struggles, and You provide for us in ways we cannot always see. We trust in Your wisdom and love, knowing that You care for each one of us.

Heavenly Father, accept these prayers for the sake of Your Son, our Saviour Jesus Christ. Amen.

And as He taught us, let us join together in the words of the Lord's Prayer, in whatever form or language you feel most comfortable.

Pai nosso que estás nos céus,
santificado seja o teu nome,
venha o teu reino,
seja feita a tua vontade,
assim na terra como no céu.

O pão nosso de cada dia dá-nos hoje,
e perdoa-nos as nossas dívidas,
assim como nós também perdoamos aos nossos devedores.

E não nos deixes cair em tentação,
mas livra-nos do mal,
pois teu é o reino, o poder e a glória para sempre. Amém.
